Complete Information About Fertilizers

Urea

It provides Nitrogen for last leaf growth. Urea should be applied in split does to avoid Nitrogen loss and get better results.

DAP

It help in strong root development and early plant growth. DAP usually applied at the time of planting to support healthy sugarcane growth.

MOP

It improves sugar content, strengthens plant stems, and increases disease resistance. MOP helps the crop tolerate drought and improves overall yield quality.

Organic Compost

Organic Compost is a natural fertilizer made from decomposed plant and animal waste.it improves soil fertility,increases water holding capacity,and enhances Soil structure.Compost supports healthy sugarcane growth by providing nutrients slowly and naturally.
